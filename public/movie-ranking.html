The Best Movies According to Fatih

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Mask

A beautiful and a really funny movie that I watched on the VHS way back in the day. It's actually one of the first movies I've ever seen in my life!

Tenacious D in The Pick of Destiny

Again, a really funny movie with one of my all time favorite actors Jack Black starring in it. It inspied me a great deal as a young musician. Still does, to this day!

Waking Life

A very philosophical movie that's also very interesting visually. It's a very creative and a very deep movie that gets you thinking!
